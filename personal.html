About me

Now, I am pursuing a doctoral degree under the guidance of my supervisor, Professor Wang.

My research primarily revolves around the potential implications and economic consequences on the behavior of mutual fund investors, the structure of fund families, and the management approach of portfolio managers. I usually employ Stata, Python, and R for conducting empirical analysis.

I obtained my bachelor's degree in Economics from Henan University in 2020 and my master's degree in Economics from Shanghai Maritime University in 2023.

I have been engaged in empirical research on capital market and have published several papers. My hobbies include watching movies, exploring different cuisines, enjoying tea, and listening to cross-talk.
